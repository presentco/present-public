Meet The Team

We’re a team of creatives rallied around a common mission: creating more connected and supportive local communities. It’s not enough to just facilitate existing communities. We hope to enable the creation of entirely new, stronger communities, thereby making our world—the real world in particular—a better place.

Bob

As CEO and co-founder of Present, Bob is responsible for the growth of Present. He has been developing game-changing software for over 20 years. He helped develop Android at Google before becoming the CTO of Square. He was one of the earliest Twitter users and has always been passionate about helping set a new standard for social networks.

When he’s not in the office you’ll find him spending time with his two daughters or swimming laps in the pool. The most influential women in his life? His mother (a former newspaper editor) and of course, his daughters!

Pat

Patrick is a co-founder of Present where he oversees app’s development. Pat is best known for writing the first ever book series on Java, titled *"Exploring Java" *and* "Learning Java,"* as well as developing the popular BeanShell Java Scripting Language.
